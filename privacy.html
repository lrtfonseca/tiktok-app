Privacy Policy

This application interacts with the TikTok API to retrieve metadata from videos and, when authorized by the user, publish video content on the user's TikTok account.

The application only accesses data from TikTok accounts that explicitly authorize the application through TikTok OAuth authentication.

The retrieved data may include video identifiers, titles, publish dates and engagement metrics.

This data may be stored in an internal database for analytics and content management purposes.

The application does not collect personal information outside the data provided by the official TikTok API.

No data is sold or shared with third parties.
